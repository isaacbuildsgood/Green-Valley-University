Year 1 — Foundations
Business fundamentals and basic accounting
  • Financial Accounting
  • Business Management

Year 2 — Core Accounting
Management and auditing principles

  • Auditing & Assurance
  • Taxation Fundamentals

  • Industry Placement
  • Advanced Accounting
  • Elective Modules

  • Advanced Auditing & Assurance
  • Professional Development
