How to Apply

Applications are accepted online through our portal. Submit your transcripts, test scores, and personal statement.

Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Letters of recommendation

Deadlines

Fall semester: June 1
Spring semester: November 1
