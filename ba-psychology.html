Course Overview

Comprehensive psychology program covering cognitive, clinical and developmental psychology with hands-on research experience.

Learning Outcomes

🧠

Understand psychological theory and research

🔬

Design and evaluate empirical studies

💬

Apply evidence-based approaches to practice

Course Structure

Year 1 — Foundations
Introduction to psychology and methods
  • Introductory Psychology
  • Research Methods
  • Statistics for Psychology
Year 2 — Core Topics
Cognitive, developmental and social psychology
  • Cognitive Psychology
  • Developmental Psychology
  • Social Psychology
Year 3 — Applied & Research
Placement options and research project
  • Research Project
  • Clinical and Community Placements
  • Advanced Electives
Year 4 — Capstone & Specialism
Final project and career preparation
  • Capstone Thesis
  • Specialist Electives
  • Professional Portfolio Development

Career Opportunities

Research & Data
