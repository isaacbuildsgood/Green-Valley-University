Faculty of Law

Education in Law, Justice, and Legal Studies

Faculty Information

Faculty Overview

The Faculty of Law at Green Valley University provides rigorous training in legal theory, practice, and ethics. Our programs prepare students for careers in law, public service, and legal advocacy, emphasizing justice, critical analysis, and professional responsibility.

Dean of the Faculty

Dean: Prof. James E. Carter

Prof. Carter is an expert in constitutional law and human rights, with a career spanning judicial clerkships and international legal advocacy. He champions justice and legal education.

"Law is the foundation of society, ensuring fairness and protecting the rights of all."
