Faculty Information

Faculty Overview

The Faculty of Business at Green Valley University cultivates entrepreneurial spirit and business acumen. Our programs in administration, finance, marketing, international business, and accounting develop skills for global business leadership and ethical decision-making.

Dean of the Faculty

Dean: Prof. Robert L. Thompson

Prof. Thompson brings decades of corporate and academic experience in finance and entrepreneurship. He has advised startups and Fortune 500 companies, emphasizing ethical business practices.

"Business is about creating value that lasts, for people, communities, and the planet."
