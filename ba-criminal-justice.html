Year 1 — Foundations
Law, criminology and research methods

Year 2 — Core Topics
Justice systems and policy studies
  • Criminal Justice Systems
  • Corrections & Rehabilitation
  • Criminal Policy
Year 3 — Applied Practice
Field placements and applied projects
  • Field Placement
  • Policy Project
  • Elective Modules

  • Capstone Research Project
  • Advanced Seminars
  • Professional Practice
