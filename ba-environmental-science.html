Course Structure

Year 1 — Foundations
Ecology, earth systems and methods
  • Introduction to Ecology
  • Earth Systems
  • Field Methods
Year 2 — Core Topics
Conservation, policy and modelling
  • Conservation Biology
  • Environmental Policy
  • Environmental Modelling
Year 3 — Applied Experience
Internships and capstone projects
  • Internship Placement

  • Specialist Electives
