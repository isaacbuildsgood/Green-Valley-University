Faculty Information

Faculty Overview

The Faculty of Medicine at Green Valley University is dedicated to training compassionate healthcare professionals. Our comprehensive programs in medicine, nursing, pharmacy, public health, and dentistry emphasize clinical excellence, research, and patient-centered care.

Dean of the Faculty

Dean: Prof. Lisa M. Wong

Prof. Wong is a renowned physician-scientist with expertise in oncology and medical education. She has pioneered new treatments and is dedicated to training compassionate healthcare professionals.

"Medicine is a calling to heal, innovate, and serve humanity with integrity and compassion."
