Faculty Information

Faculty Overview

The Faculty of Arts at Green Valley University is dedicated to fostering critical thinking, creativity, and cultural understanding. Our programs span literature, history, philosophy, psychology, and sociology, preparing students to engage with complex societal issues and contribute meaningfully to the world.

Dean of the Faculty

Dean: Prof. Emily A. Johnson

Prof. Johnson has over 20 years of experience in literary studies and has published numerous works on contemporary poetry.
She is passionate about interdisciplinary approaches to arts education.

"The arts teach us to see the world through different lenses, fostering empathy and innovation."

Our Courses

English Literature

Delve into classic and contemporary literature, developing analytical and writing skills.

History

Explore historical events and their impact on modern society.

Psychology

Study human behavior and mental processes through scientific inquiry.

Sociology

Examine social structures, institutions, and human interactions.

Philosophy

Engage with fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, and ethics.

Theatre Arts

Develop performance, design, and directing skills through practical and theoretical study of theatre arts.
