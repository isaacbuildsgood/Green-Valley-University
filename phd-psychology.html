Course Overview

Doctoral-level research training in cognitive, clinical or developmental psychology under close supervision.

Learning Outcomes

🔬

Prepare for academic and professional leadership

Program Structure

Methods training and proposal development
  • Advanced Methods

Independent research culminating in a thesis
  • Thesis Research

Senior clinical roles (with registration)
