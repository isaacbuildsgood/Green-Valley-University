Faculty Information

Faculty Overview

The Faculty of Science at Green Valley University is committed to scientific discovery and innovation. Our programs in mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science equip students with the tools to address global challenges through research and experimentation.

Dean of the Faculty

Dean: Prof. Michael K. Chen

Prof. Chen is a leading researcher in quantum physics with over 25 years in academia. He has led groundbreaking projects in particle physics and is committed to advancing scientific discovery.

"Science is not just about answers, but about asking the right questions that change the world."
